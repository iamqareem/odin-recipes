Nyama Choma Recipe

Nyama-Choma boasts a name among street food pundits across every street corner of most towns in Uganda

It is done most so often and never misses any occasion. It's literally the street culture of the land

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Apple Cider vinegar
  • Sticky marinade
  • Salt

Simple steps

  1. Chop the beef into very sizeable chunks and hook the chunks on sticks
  2. Marinate the beef with the Sticky marinade and salt, then set to settle for around and hour.
  3. Sprinkle oil on the meat as you roast it on a burning stove.
  4. Roast all the meat sticks till ready and serve
