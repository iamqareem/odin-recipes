Coconut Rice Recipe

Coconut Rice is one of the most sought festive dishes among Ugandans. A recipe idea crafted and perfected through intermarriages from the cost found it's way into the vast land.

Many households are witnessed to prepare this dish most occasionally on ceremonies and dinners.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Salt
  • Coconut Milk
  • Non-fat oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic cloves

Simple steps

  1. Boil a reasonable amount of coconut milk and set aside to cool for a while.
  2. Chop the onions and garlic sizably and fry them to golden yellow
  3. Pour 1/2 kilo of rice into the golden-yello mixture and stir until the rice adopts the smell of the mixture.
  4. Pour two measuring cups of coconut milk into the rice
  5. Add salt and stir
  6. Pour the entire mixture into a pressure cooker or ric-cooker until ready
  7. The coconut milk rice is ready
