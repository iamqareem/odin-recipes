Rolex Recipe

The Rolex is basically the most iconic street-food in Uganda crafted primarily from Eastern Uganda.

Stalls and vendor Kiosks are seen all round every town and trading centre.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Non-fat oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions

Simple steps

  1. Whisk a single egg in a single measuring cup of wehat flour until dissolved
  2. Dissolve a desired amount of salt into water and knead the dough dropwise with the water until just soft.
  3. Simultaneously smeer oil on the dough as you knead. Let to settle when soft and the dough isn't sticky
  4. Make regular doghtnut balls until all the dough has been rolled into balls. You can spare some.
  5. Spread the dough round with a rolling-pin and shallow fry on a pan till the roserie is ready.
  6. Spread and shallow fry all the dough balls.
  7. Whiskey a couple of eggs with some tomatoes and onions in them and shallow fry them too.
  8. Spread one of the fried roseries over the ready eggs and roll them together.
  9. Our rolex is ready for serving
